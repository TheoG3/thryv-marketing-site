## Week 3 — Reputation / Reviews
**Source post:** How Many Google Reviews Do You Need to Beat Your Competition?

---

A 4.7 rating with 80 reviews beats a 5.0 rating with 15 reviews. Every time.

Here's why: consumers don't trust perfection. A perfect 5.0 with few reviews looks like the business is filtering who gets to leave feedback. A 4.7 with real volume — including a few 4-star reviews responded to professionally — looks like a real operation with real customers.

The sweet spot according to local search data: 4.4 to 4.8 stars, with reviews from the last 90 days carrying the most weight.

Review count matters less than review velocity. 20 reviews from this month outperform 80 reviews from 2022 in Google's algorithm.

If your competitor got more reviews than you in the last 90 days, they're pulling ahead on a dimension that has nothing to do with service quality.
